# References

ECHOWALL stands on a lot of shoulders. If our work is useful to you, please cite us *and* the prior work below.

---

## Foundational CSI work

- Halperin, D., Hu, W., Sheth, A., & Wetherall, D. (2011). **Tool Release: Gathering 802.11n Traces with Channel State Information.** *ACM SIGCOMM CCR.*
- Schulz, M., Gürses, S., Hollick, M. (2019). **nexmon: The C-based Firmware Patching Framework.** Software Release, TU Darmstadt.
- Yang, Z., Zhou, Z., Liu, Y. (2013). **From RSSI to CSI: Indoor Localization via Channel Response.** *ACM Computing Surveys.*

## Wi-Fi sensing surveys

- Ma, Y., Zhou, G., Wang, S. (2019). **WiFi Sensing with Channel State Information: A Survey.** *ACM Computing Surveys.*
- Hernandez, S. M., & Bulut, E. (2023). **WiFi Sensing on the Edge: Signal Processing Techniques and Challenges.** *IEEE Communications Surveys & Tutorials.*

## Through-wall & vital-signs

- Adib, F., Hsu, C.-Y., Mao, H., Katabi, D., Durand, F. (2015). **Capturing the Human Figure Through a Wall.** *ACM SIGGRAPH Asia.*
- Liu, J., Wang, Y., Chen, Y., Yang, J., Chen, X., Cheng, J. (2015). **Tracking Vital Signs During Sleep Leveraging Off-the-shelf WiFi.** *ACM MobiHoc.*
- Zhao, M., Adib, F., Katabi, D. (2016). **Emotion Recognition Using Wireless Signals.** *ACM MobiCom.*

## RF-based pose

- Zhao, M., Li, T., Abu Alsheikh, M., Tian, Y., Zhao, H., Torralba, A., Katabi, D. (2018). **Through-Wall Human Pose Estimation Using Radio Signals (RF-Pose).** *CVPR.*
- Wang, F., Zhou, S., Panev, S., Han, J., Huang, D. (2019). **Person-in-WiFi: Fine-grained Person Perception using WiFi.** *ICCV.*

## Acoustic sensing

- Wang, W., Liu, A. X., Sun, K. (2016). **Device-free Gesture Tracking Using Acoustic Signals.** *ACM MobiCom.*
- Mao, W., He, J., Qiu, L. (2016). **CAT: High-Precision Acoustic Motion Tracking.** *ACM MobiCom.*

## Public datasets we use or benchmark against

- **Widar 3.0** — Tsinghua. Cross-domain gesture set.
- **SignFi** — sign-language CSI dataset.
- **CSI-HAR** — human activity recognition.

## Privacy & federated learning

- Abadi, M. et al. (2016). **Deep Learning with Differential Privacy.** *ACM CCS.*
- McMahan, B. et al. (2017). **Communication-Efficient Learning of Deep Networks from Decentralized Data (FedAvg).** *AISTATS.*
- Hu, E. J. et al. (2021). **LoRA: Low-Rank Adaptation of Large Language Models.** *ICLR 2022.*

## How to cite ECHOWALL

Until we publish a preprint, please use:

```bibtex
@software{echowall2026,
  author       = {Alshammari, Sulaiman and contributors},
  title        = {ECHOWALL: Open-source Wi-Fi CSI and Acoustic Fusion Radar},
  year         = {2026},
  url          = {https://github.com/Khawrzm/echowall},
  version      = {0.1.0}
}
```

A proper arXiv preprint is on the v0.2 roadmap.
